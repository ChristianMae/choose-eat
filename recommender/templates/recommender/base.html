We Choose what you Eat!



  • About

  • Choose-Eat provides restaurant recommendations that you will surely love!

  • Preference-based

    Our recommendations take not only price levels and distance into consideration, but also a wide variety of food categories that you like and dislike so we can ensure that you'd be satisfied with the results.


    Social

    Who doesn't love going out with friends? Well we all know how these times can be plagued with the question, "Where are we eating?". Fret not! Our recommendations are not limited to just individuals, but available for groups too!


    Location-based

    Results are based on your location and your specified distance limitation. The app is also helpful when traveling to other places because we provide directions to the establishments we recommend.
