  • dock  | /däk/ | verb |
    - to cut short
    - to take away a part of
    (From Merriam-Webster's 3rd definition)

    Dock is basically yet another URL shortener, similar to Bitly.

    Turn a long URL like this:
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QslJYDX3o8s&list=PLBDeA3o056yyMUiRG3xCXaSDan_bGG-Ho

    Into something like this:
    http://dockr.herokuapp.com/a5dfmp

    This way, links are more manageable, easier to share, and kept secret.
    Dock is a school project, made with the use of Django and MaterializeCSS.
